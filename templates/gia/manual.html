Introduction

Welcome to the GIA system. This platform manages attendance, schedules, and employee records efficiently.

Employee Guide

Clocking In & Out

To clock in, navigate to the dashboard and click the "Clock In" button. To clock out, click "Clock Out."

Attendance Records

Employees can view their attendance logs and report discrepancies to their supervisor.

Administrator Guide

Managing Employees

Admins can add, edit, or remove employees from the system.

Attendance Oversight

Admins can monitor attendance, force clock-out, and generate reports.

Rules & Regulations

  • Employees must clock in and out on time.
  • Unauthorized absences will be recorded and reported.
  • Admins must ensure data accuracy and confidentiality.

Support

For assistance, contact the IT department or refer to the help section in the dashboard.
